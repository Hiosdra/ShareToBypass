# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: November 3, 2025**

## Data Collection

**Share to Bypass** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

This app:
- Does not track your browsing activity
- Does not collect any personal information
- Does not store any data on our servers
- Does not share any information with third parties
- Does not use analytics or tracking tools

When you download this app from Google Play, Google may automatically collect certain information as part of their standard app distribution practices. This includes:
- Device information
- Installation data
- Crash reports (if enabled in your device settings)

This data collection is handled entirely by Google Play and is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). We do not have access to or control over this data.

## Permissions

The app only requests the minimum permissions necessary to function:
